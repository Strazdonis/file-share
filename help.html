FAQ

Why use TransferNow?

We offer a secure, fast and reliable service to easily send and share your files with your friends, family and co-workers.

Our privacy policy is clear: we do not sell or distribute our users' data.

How long will my files remain online?

Your files will be online for 24 hours.

Why upload is so slow?

We do not limit your files transfer speed. If the transfer is slow, it has probably to do with your internet connection.

Do I need a TransferNow account to download files?

You can use FILE TRANSFER for free and without registration whether you upload or download files.

I have a question that isn’t covered in this FAQ!

We get that, just reach out to our Support team and we’ll help you out!
